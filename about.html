Behind the Art

Hello! I'm Lee Ann Raven, a digital illustrator and aspiring game developer studying at San Sebastian College – Recoletos de Cavite. Based in the Philippines, I specialize in bringing fantasy worlds to life through vibrant colors, intricate character designs, and immersive storytelling.

My journey began with traditional sketching in the back of my notebooks, but I quickly fell in love with the limitless possibilities of digital art. Whether it's designing RPG characters, coding game logic, or creating cozy lo-fi scenes, I pour my heart into every pixel.
